About Eternal Storytellers

Located in the heart of Hollywood, Eternal Storytellers is a fraternity for aspiring filmmakers, film enthusiasts, and creative thinkers. We celebrate the art of filmmaking, focusing on collaboration, tradition, and innovation in cinema. Our members include technically skilled creators, film critics, and future industry leaders.

Our Activities

We offer a variety of activities centered around film and storytelling, such as:

  • Weekly movie time:
    • Monday - Sci-Fi
    • Tuesday - Thriller
    • Thursday - Romance
  • Short Film Creation: Realize different types of films with roles in camera, scriptwriting, and acting. Films are published and entered into prestigious competitions.
  • Themed Costume Parties: Attend costume parties dressed as characters from your favorite movies, with fraternities and sororities invited!

Opportunities for Members

Eternal Storytellers provides numerous opportunities for members, including:

  • Professional networking and career development
  • Participation in film culture and prestigious competitions
  • Collaborating with other members on exciting projects
  • Learning from renowned figures in the film industry, such as directors and actors

Notable alumni: Tom Cruise, David Fincher, Johnny Depp, Steven Spielberg, and more.

Admission Process

To join Eternal Storytellers, candidates must pass an admission test consisting of multiple-choice questions, a written component, and an interview. The passing criteria include achieving at least 60% in each part. Only one attempt is allowed per cycle.
